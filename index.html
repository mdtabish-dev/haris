The Significance of 13th Rajab

13th Rajab is a momentous day in Islamic history, marking the birth anniversary of Hazrat Ali ibn Abi Talib (R.A), the cousin and son-in-law of Prophet Muhammad (S.A.W).

Born in the holy Kaaba, Hazrat Ali (R.A) was the first male to accept Islam and is revered for his unwavering faith, profound wisdom, and exceptional courage.

"The most complete gift of God is a life based on knowledge."

- Hazrat Ali (R.A)
